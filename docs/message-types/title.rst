Title Messages
==============

Example
--------------

.. code-block:: yaml

  Messages:
    1:
         Title: "Hello"
         SubTitle: "World"
         Fade-In: 10
         Stay: 80
         Fade-Out: 10


Parameters
------------------------

Title
^^^^^
The title to send.

SubTitle
^^^^^^^^
The sub-title to send.

Fade-In
^^^^^^^
How long it takes the title to fade in, in ticks.

Stay
^^^^
How long the title will shown, in ticks.

Fade-Out
^^^^^^^^
How long it takes the title to fade out, in ticks.
